Early Mesos Farming

Build a steady income before you spend on anything.

Train on level-appropriate maps with stable mob density — the goal is a reliable stream of equipment drops, not the fastest possible experience. Every piece of unused equipment should go through Ardentmill extraction rather than the trash chute; item crystals hold value the raw gear doesn't.

Permanent systems to lean on

  • Maple Auction — sell item crystals and leftover materials for steady Mesos.
  • Ardentmill (Alchemy) — extraction and material conversion, free and repeatable.
  • Daily bossing — Glowing Soul Crystal drops convert directly into Mesos.
Steady beats fast. Regular farming, consistent extraction, and checking market prices before selling will outperform any shortcut.

Efficient Gear Progression

A structured upgrade path built from drops, not purchases.

Every upgrade in this path comes from naturally obtained gear, boss drops, or Mesos-based systems — no @Cash checkpoints anywhere along the way.

Early-term

Fensalir equipment and drops from the expedition bosses you can already clear.

Mid-term

Main character: lv200 Arcaneshade hat, shoes, gloves, cape, shoulder decoration; lv150 Root Abyss top and bottom.

Long-term

Gradual Mesos-funded upgrades through in-game enhancement systems.

Don't skip the account-wide layers

Maple Union and link skills strengthen every character on the account at once — they're free, permanent, and compound with everything above.
